About

Sweet Panda was founded in 2020, by Pandora Powers in Sweetcandy, California. Pandora started with a dream working out of her small kitchen selling to local churches and donating to children homes throughout the West Coast. Presently, Sweet Panda has 5 locations all over Southern California, and can ship worldwide. Sweet Panda makes the best macaroons in the world.
